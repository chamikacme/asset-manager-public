How it works

It’s as simple as it sounds and ever more!

1. Create an organization

Sign up and create an organization to start managing your assets. You'll be the admin of the organization and can invite other users to join.

2. Add your assets

Add your organization assets to the system. You can add details such as asset name, description, condition, and more.

3. Assign and track assets

Assign assets to users and track their status. Members can only view their assets and updates.
